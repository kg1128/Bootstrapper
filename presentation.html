The Shift

Two Eras of AI

Phase 1 — Helper
AI assists your work
  • Summarize documents, draft emails
  • Coding assistant — suggest snippets
  • Human still owns architecture & logic
  • Value: saves minutes per task
"Summarize this credit memo in 3 bullets"
Phase 2 — Agent
AI builds the solution
  • You define the what — AI owns the how
  • Writes the model, code, UI, exports — iteratively
  • Value: speed, cost, expanded capabilities
"Build an optimizer that maximizes yield subject to duration and BSCR constraints, with an interactive UI"

"Not just faster typing — a fundamentally different division of labor."
